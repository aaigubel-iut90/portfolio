Orange by the numbers


Welcome to the economy page.

This page aims to describe Orange's economy.
Orange's revenue in 2023 amounted to €43.47 billion.
Orange's revenue is generally higher than other companies in the industry,
however, Orange's revenue has steadily declined between 2014 and 2020, dropping from 23,763 to 22,603 in 2020,
which represents a decrease of approximately 4.9 percentage points.
Although Orange holds a significant share of the French and broader European telecommunications market,
it is not the leading company in terms of the number of global subscribers, as shown in the following ranking.
